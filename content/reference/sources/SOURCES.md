# Source Curricula — Citations

This domain's core is synthesized from three goalkeeping curricula. The
**source PDFs are held by the maintainer and are NOT redistributed in this
repository** — they are third-party copyrighted coaching documents. They
are cited here as provenance; the synthesized core in this Element
(`PHILOSOPHY.md`, `framework/`, `syllabus/`, `drills/`, `vocabulary.md`) is
transformative work derived from them.

1. **Developing a Training Program for Coaching Goalkeeping** ("The GK
   Trainer") — Rob Walker, US National Goalkeeping Staff Coach.
   *Anchors:* the technical syllabus, the two training methods, the
   U12–U19 load bands.
2. **ECNL Goalkeeping Curriculum & Handbook, First Edition** — Birmingham
   United Soccer Association (BUSA), ECNL program.
   *Anchors:* the philosophy ("goalkeepers are soccer's great
   contrarians"), the four-stage communication syllabus, tactical
   situations, mentality.
3. **Goalkeeper Plan 2015–2016** — youth-club goalkeeper development
   program.
   *Anchors:* organization, the U9–U18 age syllabus, the named-drill
   library, testing.

See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for how each source maps into the core.

> Maintainer note: the original PDFs live locally under
> `content/reference/sources/` (gitignored) and are the working source of
> record. To work from them, place the PDFs back in this directory.
